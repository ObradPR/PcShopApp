The most popular categories

Processor

Processors, often referred to as the "brain" of a computer, play a crucial role in determining overall system performance. They are responsible for executing instructions and calculations, influencing tasks such as multitasking, gaming, content creation, and running demanding software applications.

Ram memory

RAM (Random Access Memory) is a crucial component of a computer system that provides temporary storage for data that the processor needs to access quickly. One key aspect of RAM is its speed, measured in megahertz (MHz) or gigahertz (GHz).

Laptops

Laptops offer portable computing power, allowing users to work, study, create, and play from anywhere. They combine performance and mobility, making them an essential tool for professionals, students, and casual users alike.
