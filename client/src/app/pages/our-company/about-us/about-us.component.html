About PcShop company



Since its foundation in 2020, the PcShop company has been one of the leaders in the computer technology market in Serbia. From a small store in Belgrade, the company has grown into one of the best chains of retail stores in Serbia, with a comprehensive offer of IT devices and so on.



During the last year, PcShop managed to position itself as a company that was one of the first to present retail establishments modeled after prestigious stores in the West, offered the most complete range of products, defined improved forms of communication with customers and launched online shopping. The company currently has over 200 employees.




Vision



The vision of the PcShop company is to provide the highest level of service on the domestic market with constant innovation and enrichment of the product range. That vision will be made with our partners, which share the same vision.




Mission



PcShop is recognized on the market as a company that encourages the application of new technologies, directing the customer to get the most for his money, especially taking care that such a purchase has its value in the future.
