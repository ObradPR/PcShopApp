We rent premises



We rent premises throughout Serbia. Premises should have the following characteristics:



  • Area from 70 to 200 m2
  • The premises must be on one level
  • The premises must have a direct entrance from the street and a minimum window of 3m in length
  • Frequency of passers-by by the premises - minimum 20,000 per day
  • Minimum distance of 1km from the existing PcShop store
  • The possibility of having a parking space for delivery is desirable
  • Two phone lines and fast internet connection
  • Minimum lease period - 2 years
  • If the premises is located in a pedestrian zone, it is necessary to be on the side of the main direction of pedestrian movement, as well as to be able to deliver goods in the morning hours.
